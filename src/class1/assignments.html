    01 - TYPOGRAPHIC AUDIT

    Inspiration, Classification, Articulation

    How do you define your style and taste as adesigner? Through this exercise you willcollect and analyze your design influences(from the internet, books, surroundings,Pinterest, Instagram, Behance, etc.) and sortthem into categories that mean something toyou. You will use these categories as the basisfor the rest of your projects in this course.


    —Collect ​at least​ 50 influences in a folder onyour computer

    —Sort your influences into self-definedcategories and give them names that meansomething to you

    —Write adjectives that describe each category

    —Share your categories in a presentation
